# DepthForge — Safety Guide

## ⚠️ Photosensitive Epilepsy Warning

**DepthForge generates high-contrast flickering patterns that may trigger photosensitive epilepsy.**

Approximately 1 in 4,000 people has photosensitive epilepsy. Stereograms, especially psychedelic patterns and rapidly changing dot fields in video sequences, can trigger seizures in susceptible individuals.

---

## Who Is at Risk

- People with diagnosed photosensitive epilepsy
- People with undiagnosed photosensitivity (estimated 3× those diagnosed)
- Anyone viewing content on a screen less than 1 metre away
- Children and teenagers (higher prevalence than adults)

Even people with no history of seizures may be affected.

---

## High-Risk Content Types

| Content | Risk level | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Psychedelic patterns (full HSV rotation) | **HIGH** | Most likely to trigger |
| High-contrast SIRDS at >10 Hz | **HIGH** | Video sequences especially |
| Stripes or checkerboard patterns | **MEDIUM** | Classic trigger patterns |
| Greyscale SIRDS — static | **LOW** | Still apply `safe_mode` for public |
| Anaglyph — static | **LOW** | Use `OPTIMISED` mode |

---

## Using Safe Mode

Always enable `safe_mode` when generating content for public display, social media, print, or broadcast.

```python
import depthforge as df

# Synthesis — limits parallax
stereo = df.synthesize(
    depth, pattern,
    df.StereoParams(safe_mode=True)
)

# Pattern generation — limits contrast to 50%
pattern = df.generate_pattern(
    df.PatternParams(safe_mode=True)
)
```

**What `safe_mode` does:**

| Setting | Normal | Safe mode |
|---|---|---|
| `depth_factor` | up to ±1.0 | clamped to ±0.5 |
| `max_parallax_fraction` | up to 0.1 | clamped to 1/30 |
| Pattern contrast | 100% | 50% |

## Video Guidelines

Video stereograms (sequences of frames) carry the highest risk.

**Rules for public-facing video:**

1. **Frame rate:** Keep stereogram animations below 3 Hz (less than 3 full depth-field changes per second).
2. **No flash sequences:** Do not cut rapidly between high-contrast stereograms.
3. **Pattern choice:** Use greyscale or low-saturation monochrome patterns only.
4. **Duration:** Limit continuous viewing to 30 seconds; include a visual break.
5. **Warning:** Display a photosensitivity warning on any stereogram video before it begins.

---

## Mandatory Warning Text

For public content (web, social, broadcast, events), include this warning:

> **PHOTOSENSITIVITY WARNING:** This content contains rapidly changing patterns and high-contrast imagery that may trigger seizures or epileptic episodes in people with photosensitive epilepsy. Viewer discretion is advised.

---

## Regulatory Standards

For broadcast and public digital signage, follow these guidelines:

- **UK/EU:** Ofcom/PEGI guidelines — no more than 3 flashes per second, relative luminance change < 10%
- **US:** FCC guidelines for broadcast; W3C WCAG 2.1 Success Criterion 2.3.1 for web
- **IEC 61966:** Colour and luminance transition limits for display standards

`safe_mode=True` is designed to comply with these standards for static images. For video, additional testing is recommended.

---

## First Aid

If someone experiences a seizure while viewing DepthForge content:

1. Keep them safe — clear the area of hard objects
2. Do not restrain them; do not put anything in their mouth
3. Turn off the display or move them away from it
4. Time the seizure
5. Call emergency services if: the seizure lasts more than 5 minutes, the person doesn't recover consciousness, or they are injured
