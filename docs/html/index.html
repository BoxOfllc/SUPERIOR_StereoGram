Output Modes

Six stereoscopic output formats supported across all integration surfaces.

👁️

SIRDS

Classic random-dot "Magic Eye" stereogram. Gaze through the surface to reveal hidden 3D depth from your depth map.

🎭

Texture Pattern

Tileable custom image as the pattern layer — logos, art, seamless textures. Pattern repeats encode the depth field.

🔒

Hidden Image

Shape or text encoded invisibly in dot noise. Reveals only when cross-eyed or parallel-fused.

🕶️

Anaglyph

Red/cyan (and 4 variants including Dubois optimized) for glasses-based 3D. Supports all display types.

⟺

Stereo Pair

Left/right views side-by-side or top/bottom. For VR headsets, 3D monitors, cross-eye or wall-eye free fusion.

🎞️

Video Sequence

Temporally stable stereogram sequences with EMA/windowed/flow-guided smoothing. Scene-cut robust.
